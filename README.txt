Secure Credit Card Payment Gateway for WordPress.

== Description ==
Tap Payments is a powerful and secure credit card payment gateway designed specifically for Woocommerce Stores. It provides a seamless integration to process online payments, allowing businesses and individuals to accept credit card transactions with ease. With Tap Payments, you can offer a smooth checkout experience to your customers while ensuring the highest level of security for their sensitive payment information. Whether you're running an e-commerce store, a membership site, or a donation platform, Tap Payments is the ideal payment gateway solution for your WordPress website.

== Features ==
* Quick and Easy Integration: Tap Payments offers a simple and hassle-free integration process. 
* Secure Payment Processing: Rest assured that your customers' payment information is handled securely. Tap Payments adheres to the highest security standards, utilizing encryption protocols to safeguard sensitive data and protect against fraudulent activities
* Multiple Payment Options: Tap Payments supports a wide range of credit cards, including Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Mada, and more. This ensures that your customers have the flexibility to choose their preferred payment method.

== Installation ==
To install this plugin:

1. Get your API Key & Private Key from [Tap Payments](https://www.tap.company/).
2. Download the plugin.
3. Upload the plugin to the /wp-content/plugins/ directory.
4. Go to "Plugins" in your WordPress admin, then click "Activate".
5. Go to "Woocommerce" in your WordPress admin then click "settings".
6. Click on "Payments" tab in woocommerce settings menu, you will find Tap Gateway click "Manage".
7. Paste the API Key and Private Key and configure the settings as you prefer.
8. Click "Save Changes" once finished.
